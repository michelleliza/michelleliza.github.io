The Other Side of COVID-19

In 2020, the world was gripped by the CoVid-19 pandemic. It impacts all facets of human life, including daily habits and lifestyle. These changes are causing other unseen effects around the world. Invisible, but significant. Here, we would like to show the positive effect that the pandemic has on the air quality of the countries in Europe.
